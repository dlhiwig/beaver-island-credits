### 1. NATURE OF ARAMORE CREDITS
I understand that Beaver Island Credits are **NOT legal tender**, NOT convertible to U.S. Dollars or any other currency, and NOT cryptocurrency. They are solely for barter exchange among members of the Beaver Island Veterans Cooperative Association ("the Cooperative").

### 2. TAX OBLIGATIONS
I understand that barter transactions may be **taxable income** under the Internal Revenue Code (IRC §6045). The Cooperative may be required to file IRS Form 1099-B reporting the fair market value of my exchanges. I am solely responsible for reporting barter income on my personal tax return and for any resulting tax liability.

### 3. VA BENEFITS ADVISORY
I acknowledge that participation in barter transactions **may affect my eligibility** for Veterans Administration (VA) benefits, including means-tested pensions. I have been advised to consult with a VA benefits counselor regarding my individual circumstances before participating. The Cooperative provides no legal, tax, or benefits advice.

### 4. BYLAWS
I have received, read, and agree to abide by the Bylaws of the Beaver Island Veterans Cooperative Association, as may be amended from time to time.

### 5. CLOSED-LOOP RESTRICTION
I agree that Beaver Island Credits are **non-transferable outside the Cooperative**. I will not attempt to sell, exchange, or convert credits for U.S. Dollars, cryptocurrency, or any other form of legal tender. Violation of this provision is grounds for immediate expulsion.

### 6. CONFIDENTIALITY
I agree to keep Cooperative activities, member identities, transaction details, and operational methods **confidential**. I will not disclose this information to non-members except as required by law.

### 7. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
Any disputes arising from my participation shall be resolved first through **internal mediation** by the Board of Directors. If mediation fails, disputes shall be submitted to **binding arbitration** under Michigan law, with costs shared equally between parties.

### 8. TERMINATION
I may voluntarily withdraw from the Cooperative by written notice to the Board. Upon withdrawal or expulsion:
- My remaining credit balance will be redeemed through available goods/services within 60 days, subject to availability
- After 60 days, any unredeemed balance is forfeited to the Cooperative
- My transaction records will be retained per the Cooperative's record-keeping policy (minimum 5 years)

### 9. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
The Cooperative, its Board of Directors, officers, and members shall not be liable for any loss, damage, or injury arising from my participation, except in cases of willful misconduct or gross negligence.

### 10. ACKNOWLEDGMENT
I acknowledge that I am joining voluntarily, that I have had the opportunity to seek independent legal counsel, and that I understand all terms of this agreement.

## ⚠️ IMPORTANT NOTICE

**ARAMORE CREDITS ARE NOT LEGAL TENDER.** They are not issued, backed, or guaranteed by any government, bank, or financial institution. They are not U.S. Dollars, cryptocurrency, digital currency, securities, or any form of money.

Beaver Island Credits are **private barter exchange tokens** used exclusively among members of the Beaver Island Veterans Cooperative Association for the exchange of goods and services. They have **no cash value**, cannot be redeemed for U.S. Dollars or any other currency, and are **non-transferable** outside the Cooperative.

Participation in the Beaver Island Credits system is voluntary. Barter transactions may be subject to federal and state income tax obligations. Members are solely responsible for their own tax compliance.

The Beaver Island Veterans Cooperative Association is a Michigan cooperative corporation organized under MCL 450.98 et seq. It is not a bank, money transmitter, or financial institution.

**By accepting, holding, or transacting in Beaver Island Credits, you acknowledge and agree to these terms.**
